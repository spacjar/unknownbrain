Chapter I

It's the year 2016. An anonymous Duo from Germany started releasing music together under the label “No Copyright Sounds”. Hiding their identity behind their trademark masks, Unknown Brains fanbase started growing because of the unique sounding style of their music. Being used in countless videos around the internet, the music of the Duo has always been experimental and difficult to put into genres. Every song of theirs is a different experience which is connected through touching melodies and unique basslines. While establishing their name in the electronic music industry Unknown Brain released under diverse labels next to “NCS”, such as “Spinnin’ Records”, “Universal Music Group” and more.

Without preparing their fans for anything the Duo burned their trademark mask and deleted every post from their socials, leaving thousands of people speechless. “Was that the end?” - “Will they ever return?” - “What happens next?”

After one year going completely silent, Unknown Brain returned like a phoenix from the ashes with new masks and their debut Album “Faceless”.

Chapter II

It's the year 2021 and 50 releases later. The new Chapter of Unknown Brain is based around the futuristic and slick new masks that they have representing their new found identity within music. Known for their anonymity, the mysterious duo look to further push their agenda of “music doesn’t need a face”. Next to the visual appearance, Unknown Brain’s transition from electronic music to a more mature and pop-leaning sound signifies the next chapter of their musical careers. Releasing the first songs of this new era under “Warner Music”, their experimental style now feels way more organic and natural.
